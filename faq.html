Are Livhea's coaches substitutes for gynaecologists?

No. Livhea's coaches are not a substitute for your gynaecologist. We recommend you must select your gynaecologist based on your parameters (ask us for selecting the right parameter by dropping an email to info@livhea.com). Our coaches only provide services which a typical gynaecologist provides beyond clinical advice but may not be able to devout enough time to an individual patient due to patient load.

Do Livhea coaches provide any clinical advice?

No. Livhea's coaches are only trained by medical practitioners but are not permitted to provide any clinical advice. They are not entitled to read any medical or diagnostic report or provide feedback on any medicine / drug recommended by your doctor.

What kind of advice do Livhea's coaches provide?

Livhea's coaches provide non-clinical advice only. However, they have been trained by a clinical team headed by a gynaecologist to provide advice which increases the chances of natural birth delivery by promoting a healthy mind and body for the mother and child

Will I have one coach to work with me or multiple coaches?

Livhea's core philosophy lies in personalized care. We provide you with one virtual health coach who is your pregnancy buddy and stays with you throughout your subscription with our platform. In case your health coach is temporarily unavailable due to unforeseen circumstances, we will provide a backup coach to work with you in the interim till your coach is back. If for some reason, your coach becomes permanently unavailable during the course of your subscription, Livhea will provide you an alternate full time coach.
