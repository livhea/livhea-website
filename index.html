Polycystic ovary disease (PCOD) is a hormonal disorder affecting millions of women and is a common cause of infertility. It sets in particularly due to insulin resistance which can also increase the risk of diabetes in future. Weight gain, extra hair or thinning hair are the major symptoms. Weight Management is the most recommended natural treatment as it leads to normal periods and helps fight depression.

Livhea's PCOD Weight Management Program has been especially developed for women unable to conceive / facing irregular periods because of being overweight and having being diagnosed with PCOD. We have a proven track record of helping women reduce their weight in a scientific manner with no harmful impact on their ability to conceive.
