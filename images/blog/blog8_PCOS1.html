1. What is PCOS/PCOD?

A lot of things are changing with modern advent of technology, changing lifestyles and environment. While a few are good, a few others have their side effects. Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) as a term exists only for last 15 years but it impacts more than 50% of women in the world today with $4 Billion spend in US alone to manage this.

Livhea healthcare exists to help people manage their chronic and lifestyle diseases with expert medical programs. This informational mini-series on PCOS/PCOD is our attempt to create awareness and share information on causes, symptoms and management of PCOS. Let’s understand what PCOS is and how can we manage this effectively.

PCOS is an endocrine disorder that is characterized by moderate enlargement of ovarian sacs. When fluid accumulates in these sacs, it is referred as cysts (PCO Disease/PCOD). While largely the reasons for PCOS are unknown, it could be because of unhealthy lifestyle, external environmental factors like hazardous chemicals/toxins or genetic. Almost, 7/10 women in fertile age are affected by PCOS.

Diagnosis for PCOS is tricky but exists and is associated with non- existent/irregular periods, unwanted hair growth, infertility, lack of ovulation, mood swings.
