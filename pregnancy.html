About the Program

India's 1st and only, expert medical doctor designed online pregnancy management consultation program

Virtual Health Coach

Get counselling on nutrition, exercise, meditation, yoga and stress management from your personal coach who’s available for you throughout your pregnancy

Personalized advice

Scientifically developed trimester wise daily diet plans to help you gain weight in right manner + get customized respones for all your questions

Trusted friend

Chat with your coach in a secured environment through Livhea’s mobile & Web app, share all your concerns and let the coach help you out

Highly Trained

Livhea’s coaches have a medical background in nutrition and are trained on all aspects of pregnancy management by our expert medical team

Availability

Your personal coach is available for you at your fingertips, from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Saturday

Motivation

Your coach will track your diet, exercise and stress levels and motivate you to improve your lifestyle to help you have a healthy body and a healthy mind

International Standards

Livhea’s coaches follow international guidelines as recommended by institutes such as World Health Organization, Childbirth and Postpartum Professional Association (CAPPA)

Traditional therapies

Our coaches also include principles of traditional therapies such as Ayurveda and Garbh Sanskar in their counselling

Normal deliveries

Livhea’s mission is to reduce C-Sec rate in India. Livhea’s coaches work with mothers to help improve their chances of normal delivery

Please note that our coaches don't provide clinical advise and only aim to provide you additional support (anytime and from anywhere) apart from your local gynaecologist and obstetrician or child birth assistant.
